Car Run Prototype

This is a prototype car obstacle course. A mix between precision driving, time navigation and dodging obstacles. At its core the player must navigate through a track filled with moving and static obstacles which requires the player to be vigilant with their awareness, timing and reflex control.

Broken Bonds

You're just a stuffed toy—but to someone, you once meant everything. Now, torn and losing stuffing, you awaken in a familiar room filled with echoes of childhood, rivalry, and memories. You play as a teddy bear caught in the middle of two brothers’ feud. Your mission? Reunite with your original owner, Brother 1, and prove you still belong by collecting lost pieces of yourself along the way.
