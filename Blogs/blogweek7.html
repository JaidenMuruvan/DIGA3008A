Week 7 - Blog Posts

I will be looking at the Makro Website.

Pros

  • There is a wide selection of products on the website with easy category browsing.
  • The search bar functionality is responsive and helps to filter the products efficiently.
  • The search bar and navigation bar all stay consistent through pages of the website.
  • The information such as contact details also stay consistent in each page, making it easily accessible.
  • The deals and promotions are highlighted for easy access, so things like this grabs the attention of the user.

Cons

  • At times the website can have poor performance such as slow loading times especially on the product pages.
  • The UI feels very cluttered and can make the user feel really overwhelmed with the excessive use of banners and ads.
  • The website is not as responsive on smaller devices as it is on a laptop.
